Study the Tarot at Codex Tarot

Welcome to the Study section of Codex Tarot, where you can deepen your understanding of the Marseilles Tarot tradition through interactive learning tools and comprehensive references.

How to Use This Section

The Study section is divided into four main areas:

  1. Visual Guide - An interactive reference that helps you explore the symbolism of each card through visual elements like colors, numbers, gestures, and objects.
  2. Interactive Lessons - Structured learning modules that guide you from basic concepts to advanced interpretations.
  3. 20 Questions Quiz - Test your knowledge with quizzes of varying difficulty to reinforce your learning.
  4. Encyclopedia - A comprehensive reference covering card meanings, historical context, symbol dictionary, and reading spreads.

Resources & Credits

The content in this section is based on the restored Jodorowsky-Camoin deck and The Way of the Tarot by Alejandro Jodorowsky.
