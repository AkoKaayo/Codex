The Codex Tarot

Welcome to Codex Tarot, your free online Marseilles Tarot reading app. Discover the ancient wisdom of the classic Marseilles Tarot, beautifully restored in the Jodorowsky-Camoin deck. Whether you're seeking personal insight, spiritual guidance, or a deeper understanding of tarot, our app offers accurate readings and learning resources for beginners and seasoned readers alike.

How to Use Codex Tarot

Getting started with your tarot reading is simple:

  1. Choose Your Spread: Select from a single card, three-card, or five-card reading using the buttons at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Set Your Intention: Enter a question or focus for your reading in the text box (e.g., "What should I focus on today?").
  3. Draw Your Cards: Press Enter or click the send button to draw your cards and reveal your reading.
  4. Explore Your Reading: View your cards and their meanings in the reading panel. Click on any card to zoom in for a closer look.
  5. Start Again: Use the "Shuffle & Start Again" button to clear your reading and begin anew.
