The Codex Tarot - Custom Reading

Welcome to the Custom Reading section of Codex Tarot. Here, you can create personalized Marseilles Tarot readings by selecting your own cards from the Jodorowsky-Camoin deck. Perfect for tailored insights and deeper exploration.

How to Use Custom Reading

Create your custom tarot reading in a few steps:

  1. Choose Your Spread: Select a single card, three-card, or five-card spread using the buttons below.
  2. Select Your Cards: Pick a card for each position by choosing between Major Arcana, Minor Arcana, Court Cards, or a random draw.
  3. Set Your Intention: Enter a question or focus for your reading in the text box.
  4. Generate Your Reading: Submit to reveal your personalized reading in the panel.
  5. Start Again: Use the "Shuffle & Start Again" button to clear and begin anew.

Why Choose Custom Reading?

Custom Reading lets you take control of your tarot experience, allowing you to select specific cards for each position in your spread. Explore the Jodorowsky-Camoin deck’s symbolism in a way that resonates with your personal journey.
