Welcome to Custom Reading in Codex Tarot

Welcome to the Custom Reading section of Codex Tarot. Here, you can create personalized tarot readings by selecting your own cards from the Jodorowsky-Camoin deck. This feature allows for a tailored exploration of the tarot's wisdom, perfect for those who want to focus on specific aspects or cards.

How to Use Custom Reading

Follow these steps to create your custom tarot reading:

  1. Choose Your Spread: Select the type of spread you want using the buttons at the bottom of the page: Single Card, Three Cards, or Five Cards. Each button corresponds to a different layout for your reading.
  2. Select Your Cards:
    • Once you've chosen a spread type, the card selection interface will appear, showing selectors for each position in the spread (e.g., "PAST / GENESIS" for a three-card spread).
    • For each position, choose the type of card you want:
      • Major Arcana: Select from a list including The Fool, The Magician, The High Priestess, etc., up to The World.
      • Minor Arcana: First, choose the suit (Swords, Pentacles, Cups, Wands), then select the number (Ace to Ten).
      • Court Cards: Choose the suit, then select the character (Page, Queen, King, Knight).
      • Random: Opt for a random card, and the system will select one for you during the reading.
    • After selecting the card type, follow the prompts to choose the specific card. Once selected, the card name and position will be displayed, and you can move to the next position.
    • Continue until all positions in your spread are filled. The interface will guide you through each step, with back buttons to revise selections if needed.
  3. Set Your Intention: After selecting all cards, a text box will appear. Enter a question or focus for your reading, such as "What should I focus on this week?" or "Guidance for my career path." This helps tailor the reading to your needs.
  4. Generate Your Reading: Click the submit button to send your selections and intention to the server. The reading panel will display, showing a loading spinner while the reading is generated.
  5. View Your Reading: Once generated, the reading panel will show the interpretation, including an introduction, synthesis of the cards, and a tarot message. The selected cards will be displayed above, and you can click on any card image to view it in a larger modal for detailed examination.
  6. Start Again: If you wish to create a new reading, use the "Shuffle & Start Again" button. This will clear all selections, reset the interface, and allow you to begin the process anew.

Tips for a Meaningful Reading

  • Take your time to select cards that resonate with your current situation or question. Consider the symbolism of each card type for deeper insights.
  • Be specific with your intention to get more precise and relevant readings. Vague questions may lead to broader, less focused interpretations.
  • Explore different spread types to see which one provides the most clarity for your query. Single cards are great for quick insights, while five-card spreads offer comprehensive guidance.
  • Use the card zoom modal to get a closer look at the card details, especially if you're unfamiliar with the Jodorowsky-Camoin deck's imagery.
  • If you're new to tarot, consider reading more about the deck at [Jodorowsky-Camoin Deck](https://en.camoin.com/tarot/-Home-en-.html) to enhance your understanding.
